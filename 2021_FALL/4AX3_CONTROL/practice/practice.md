## Q1

$y(x) = ax + b$

M points in the model

Linear model --> $y(x) = ax + b$ --> $Y = A\theta$

Y = $\begin{bmatrix} y_1 \\ y_2 \\ ... \\ y_M \end{bmatrix}$

A = $\begin{bmatrix} x_1 & 1 \\ x_2 & 1 \\ ... & ... \\ x_M & 1 \end{bmatrix}$

$\theta = \begin{bmatrix} a \\ b \end{bmatrix}$

$A\theta = Y$
